- **Driver drops you off:** Main entrance (Tha Chang Gate)
- **Opening:** 8:30 AM (open all day, but mid-morning is good timing)
- **Entry Fee:** 500 baht (~$14 USD) per person
- **Duration:** 2-2.5 hours
- **Dress Code:** Long pants/skirt, shoulders covered
- **‚ö†Ô∏è Scam Alert:** Ignore anyone saying palace is "closed"

- **Driver:** Waits nearby, handles parking
- **Call driver:** When finished (around 12:30 PM)

**12:30 PM - Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha)**
- **Driver picks you up:** Grand Palace entrance
- **Driver drops you off:** Wat Pho entrance (2-minute drive, or walk 10 min)
- **Entry Fee:** 200 baht (~$6 USD)
- **Duration:** 1-1.5 hours
- **Highlights:** 46-meter Reclining Buddha, traditional Thai massage school
- **Optional:** 30-min Thai massage (420 baht) - great break!
- **Why:** Birthplace of traditional Thai massage, peaceful temple grounds
- **Driver:** Waits nearby
- **Call driver:** When ready (around 2:00 PM)

**2:00 PM - Lunch Break**
- **Driver picks you up:** Wat Pho entrance
- **Options:**
  - **The Sixth** - Popular restaurant (closed Saturdays, open Monday)
  - **Elephant Coffee** - Great food and coffee
  - **Vivy the Coffee Place** - Stunning river views (driver drops you off)
  - **Street food stalls** - Quick and authentic Thai food
- **Driver drops you off:** At restaurant location
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Cost:** 100-300 baht per person
- **Call driver:** When finished lunch (around 3:00 PM)

**3:00 PM - Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn)**
- **Driver picks you up:** Restaurant location
- **Driver drops you off:** Wat Arun entrance (10-15 min drive)
- **Entry Fee:** 100 baht (~$3 USD)
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Why:** Iconic temple, best lighting in late afternoon (perfect timing!)
- **Highlights:** Climb central prang for panoramic river views, beautiful porcelain decorations
- **Driver:** Waits nearby (no parking worries!)
- **Call driver:** When ready (around 4:00 PM)

**4:00 PM - Jim Thompson House**
- **Driver picks you up:** Wat Arun entrance
- **Driver drops you off:** Jim Thompson House entrance (20-30 min drive)
- **Entry Fee:** 200 baht (~$6 USD)
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Opening Hours:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (closes at 6 PM)
- **Why:** Traditional teak houses, lush gardens, Thai silk history
- **Perfect Timing:** Air-conditioned break, beautiful gardens, educational
- **Driver:** Waits nearby
- **Call driver:** When ready (around 5:00 PM)

**5:00 PM - Return to St. Regis**
- **Driver picks you up:** Jim Thompson House entrance
- **Driver drops you off:** St. Regis Bangkok
- **Drive Time:** 15-20 minutes
- **Arrival:** ~5:20 PM
- **Total Driver Time:** ~6 hours (10:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
