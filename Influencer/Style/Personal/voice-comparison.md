# Voice Comparison: Your Style vs. Influencers

This document shows how your personal writing voice combines and adapts elements from Allie K. Miller and Dharmesh Shah.

---

## Element Comparison

### Brevity

**Dharmesh Shah:**
- Ultra-concise (often under 80 words)
- Standalone insights
- Minimal context needed

**Allie K. Miller:**
- Medium-length posts (500-1500 words)
- More context and explanation
- Structured frameworks

**Your Voice:**
- **Balanced approach:** 50-150 words for LinkedIn (shorter than Allie, slightly longer than Dharmesh's ultra-short)
- **Context when needed:** Add brief context for research-backed insights
- **Frameworks for longer content:** Use Allie's structured approach for medium posts

**Why:** You combine Dharmesh's brevity with Allie's structure, adapting length to content type.

---

### Frameworks

**Allie K. Miller:**
- Extensive use of numbered frameworks
- Detailed step-by-step guides
- Clear implementation instructions

**Dharmesh Shah:**
- Simple frameworks (like 3 P's)
- Minimal structure
- Quick, memorable patterns

**Your Voice:**
- **Structured frameworks:** Like Allie, but research-backed
- **Simple when possible:** Like Dharmesh's memorable patterns
- **Research methodology:** Your unique addition - show how you developed the framework

**Why:** You take Allie's structured approach but add your research methodology strength.
