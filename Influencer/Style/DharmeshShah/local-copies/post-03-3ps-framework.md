## Full Post Text

Here's my simple framework for judging ideas:

1) Potential: If it worked, how big could it be?
2) Probability: What are the chances it'll work?
3) Proximity: How close is this to things I care about, know about, or am passionate about?

## Framework Breakdown

### 1. Potential
- **Question:** If it worked, how big could it be?
- **Purpose:** Assess the upside/impact
- **Application:** Evaluate maximum possible value

### 2. Probability
- **Question:** What are the chances it'll work?
- **Purpose:** Assess likelihood of success
- **Application:** Realistic assessment of odds

### 3. Proximity
- **Question:** How close is this to things I care about, know about, or am passionate about?
- **Purpose:** Assess alignment with interests/expertise
- **Application:** Personal fit and motivation
