# St. Regis Bangkok Dining Options - Comprehensive Research Report

**Research Date:** December 30, 2025  
**Research Methodology:** Multi-source web research focusing on hotel restaurants and nearby dining options  
**Location:** St. Regis Bangkok, Ratchadamri Road, Bangkok, Thailand

---

## Executive Summary

The St. Regis Bangkok offers a diverse array of dining options within the hotel, ranging from a Michelin-starred fine dining restaurant to casual bars and lounges. The hotel is strategically located in the vibrant Ratchadamri area, providing easy access to Bangkok's renowned culinary scene, including nearby shopping centers, street food areas, and world-class restaurants.

---

## On-Site Dining at St. Regis Bangkok

### Fine Dining Restaurants

#### IGNIV Bangkok ⭐ (One Michelin Star)
- **Cuisine:** Modern European cuisine designed for sharing
- **Chef:** Head Chef Arne Riehn (also referenced as David Hartwig in some sources)
- **Location:** Within St. Regis Bangkok
- **Highlights:**
  - One Michelin-starred restaurant
  - Unique fine dining sharing experience
  - Contemporary European cuisine with seasonal ingredients
  - Features a renowned "candy store" with an assortment of petit fours
  - Playful approach to modern European flavors
- **Price Range:** Premium fine dining
- **Reservations:** Recommended (Michelin-starred restaurant)

#### VIU
- **Cuisine:** Mediterranean-inspired cuisine
- **Chef:** Chef Matteo Fontana
- **Location:** 12th floor of St. Regis Bangkok
- **Highlights:**
  - Panoramic views of Bangkok skyline
  - Seasonal menus featuring Mediterranean dishes
  - Signature dishes include:
    - Grilled Octopus Salad
    - Burrata & Tomato Salad
    - Handmade Pappardelle with Lamb Ragout
    - Homemade Braised Duck Leg Ravioli
  - Hosts Signature Sunday Brunch featuring specialties like:
    - Stir-Fried Wild Boar with Thai Herbs
    - Venison Carpaccio
  - "Mediterranean Quartet" four-course dining series (available January 1 to March 31, 2025)
- **Price Range:** Moderate to high
- **Views:** Panoramic city views from 12th floor

#### Zuma
- **Cuisine:** Contemporary Japanese Izakaya
- **Location:** Ground floor of St. Regis Bangkok
- **Highlights:**
  - Internationally renowned Japanese restaurant
  - Inspired by traditional Japanese Izakaya
  - Elegant yet casual dining experience
  - Indoor-outdoor setting with vibrant atmosphere
  - Menu features:
    - Sushi and sashimi
    - Robata-grilled items
    - Authentic Japanese flavors with contemporary twist
- **Price Range:** Moderate to high
- **Atmosphere:** Casual elegance, vibrant

### Bars & Lounges

#### The St. Regis Bar
- **Type:** New York-inspired lobby bar
- **Highlights:**
  - Signature St. Regis Bloody Mary (known as "Siam Mary")
  - Selection of premier and unique spirits
  - Classic cocktails
  - Champagne and caviar service
  - Breathtaking sunset views
  - Ideal for afternoon tea or evening cocktails
  - Selection of pastries and sandwiches
- **Atmosphere:** Refined, sophisticated

#### The Drawing Room
- **Type:** Afternoon tea venue
- **Highlights:**
  - Refined setting for Afternoon Tea
  - Premium teas and coffees
  - European-style pastries and sandwiches
  - Sweet and savory bites
- **Best For:** Afternoon tea experience

#### The Lounge
- **Type:** Casual lounge
- **Highlights:**
  - Perfect for informal business meetings
  - Premium coffee and tea collection
  - Handcrafted cakes, sandwiches, and pastries
  - Light dishes and desserts
  - Evening cocktails available
  - Relaxed atmosphere
- **Best For:** Morning coffee, casual meetings, evening drinks

#### Pool Bar
- **Type:** Poolside bar
- **Location:** Beside outdoor pool
- **Highlights:**
  - Fresh and healthy options
  - Fruit smoothies
  - Crisp salads
  - Asian delicacies
  - Panoramic views of Bangkok
- **Best For:** Poolside dining, healthy options

## Nearby Dining Options

### Fine Dining Restaurants Near St. Regis Bangkok

#### Paste Bangkok ⭐
- **Cuisine:** Modern Thai cuisine
- **Michelin Status:** One Michelin Star
- **Location:** Gaysorn Village shopping center (short walk from St. Regis)
- **Highlights:**
  - Michelin-starred Thai restaurant
  - Modern interpretation of traditional Thai cuisine
  - Royal Thai cuisine with century-old cooking techniques
- **Distance:** Walking distance from St. Regis

#### Gaggan ⭐⭐
- **Cuisine:** Progressive Indian cuisine
- **Michelin Status:** Two Michelin Stars
- **Highlights:**
  - Ranked #6 in The World's 50 Best Restaurants (2025)
  - #1 Best Restaurant in Asia (2025)
  - Innovative Indian fusion cuisine
  - 22-course multisensory dining experience
- **Distance:** Short drive from St. Regis

#### Erawan Tea Room
- **Cuisine:** Thai and international dishes
- **Location:** Erawan Bangkok shopping complex
- **Highlights:**
  - Blend of Thai and international cuisine
  - Sophisticated setting
  - Located in shopping complex
- **Distance:** Walking distance from St. Regis

### Shopping Center Dining

The St. Regis Bangkok is located near major shopping centers that offer numerous dining options:

#### CentralWorld
- **Distance:** Short walk or BTS ride
- **Dining Options:** Multiple restaurants, food courts, cafes
- **Highlights:** Wide variety of cuisines, casual to fine dining

#### Siam Paragon
- **Distance:** Short walk or BTS ride
- **Dining Options:** Extensive food court and restaurants
- **Highlights:** Gourmet market, international restaurants

#### Gaysorn Village
- **Distance:** Walking distance
- **Dining Options:** Includes Paste Bangkok and other restaurants
- **Highlights:** Upscale shopping center with fine dining

### Street Food & Local Areas

#### Banthat Thong Road
- **Distance:** Approximately 2 kilometers from St. Regis
- **Highlights:**
  - Renowned for vibrant street food scene
  - Ranked as world's 14th coolest street by Time Out (2024)
  - Variety of local Thai dishes and snacks
  - Popular with both locals and tourists
- **Best For:** Authentic street food experience

#### Silom Road Area
- **Distance:** Short distance from St. Regis
- **Highlights:**
  - Renowned for vibrant street food scene
  - Late-night eats available
  - Specialties include:
    - Kao Tum (boiled rice porridge)
    - Moo Grob (crispy pork)
- **Best For:** Late-night street food

## Practical Information

### Reservations
- **IGNIV Bangkok:** Highly recommended (Michelin-starred, often requires advance booking)
- **VIU:** Recommended, especially for Sunday Brunch
- **Zuma:** Recommended for dinner
- **Nearby Michelin restaurants:** Book weeks or months in advance

### Best Times to Visit
- **Fine dining restaurants:** Dinner reservations typically 6-9 PM
- **Street food areas:** Evenings (5 PM onwards) for best atmosphere
- **The St. Regis Bar:** Sunset time for best views
- **Afternoon Tea:** 2-5 PM at The Drawing Room

### Dress Codes
- **Fine dining restaurants:** Smart casual to formal (check individual restaurant requirements)
- **Hotel bars and lounges:** Smart casual
- **Street food:** Casual (no dress code)

### Payment
- **Hotel restaurants:** Credit cards accepted
- **Street food:** Cash preferred (carry smaller bills)

### Transportation
- **Walking distance:** Gaysorn Village, Erawan Bangkok, CentralWorld, Siam Paragon
- **BTS Skytrain:** Ratchadamri Station nearby (access to city-wide dining)
- **Short drive:** Banthat Thong Road, Silom Road, other fine dining restaurants
