## Executive Summary

CoreWeave, Inc. (CRWV) is a rapidly growing cloud computing company specializing in GPU-accelerated infrastructure for AI workloads. The company went public in March 2025 and has secured massive contracts with Meta ($14.2B), OpenAI ($12B), and Microsoft. Despite impressive revenue growth ($1.9B in 2024, $4.31B TTM), the company remains unprofitable with significant losses ($863M in 2024, $824.73M TTM). The stock trades at $76.42 (as of Dec 26, 2025) with a market cap of $38.08B, representing a forward-looking valuation based on future growth potential rather than current profitability.

**Investment Recommendation:** **HOLD**  
**Confidence Level:** **Medium**

1. Massive contract backlog ($26B+ with Meta and OpenAI)
2. Rapid revenue growth trajectory ($8B projected for 2025)
3. Strategic position in AI infrastructure boom
**Key Risks:**
1. Significant losses despite high revenue ($824M TTM loss)

### Business Description

CoreWeave operates a Kubernetes-native cloud platform that provides GPU-accelerated infrastructure specifically tailored for artificial intelligence (AI) and high-performance computing (HPC) workloads. The company builds infrastructure that supports compute workloads for enterprises, offering:

**Products & Services:**
- GPU compute (Nvidia H100, GB200, GB300 systems)
- CPU compute
- Storage services
- Networking services
- Managed services
- Virtual and bare metal servers
- VFX and rendering
- AI model training
- AI inference
- Mission Control software platform

**Infrastructure:**
- 32 data centers across U.S. and Europe
- Over 250,000 GPUs deployed
- Focus on latest Nvidia GPU technology (H100, GB200, GB300)

**Key Differentiator:** Specialized GPU infrastructure optimized for AI workloads, positioned as an alternative to hyperscalers (AWS, Azure, GCP) with better performance and pricing for AI-specific use cases.
