## Appendix

### Methodology Notes

**Follower Count Considerations:**
- Follower counts are approximate and change over time
- Some influencers may have private follower counts
- Numbers reflect data available at time of research
- Focus on influence quality, not just quantity

**Selection Criteria:**
- LinkedIn follower count
- Recognition (LinkedIn Top Voices, industry awards)
- Content quality and consistency
- Engagement and community building
- Real-world expertise and achievements

**Limitations:**
- Lists may vary by publication and criteria
- Some highly influential tech leaders may have fewer followers but high engagement
- Focus primarily on English-language content creators
- Lists may not include all influential tech voices

### Key Terms

- **LinkedIn Top Voices:** LinkedIn's recognition program for top content creators
- **LinkedIn Influencers:** Approximately 500 professionals invited by LinkedIn to publish (legacy program)
- **Engagement Rate:** Measure of likes, comments, and shares relative to followers
- **Thought Leadership:** Establishing expertise and influence through content creation
